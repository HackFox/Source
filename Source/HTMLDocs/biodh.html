Doug Hennig, with son Nicholas, at VFP DevConnections in Orlando, 2001.

Developer's Choice awards for both Stonefield Database Toolkit and Stonefield Reports.

 

Doug Hennig is a partner with Stonefield Systems Group Inc. He is the author of the Stonefield Database Toolkit (SDT), which won the Developer's Choice award for Best Utility at the 2000 VFP DevConnections conference in New Orleans. He is co-author of Stonefield Reports, which won the Developer's Choice award for Best Reporting Tool at the same conference.

 

Doug is a co-author (along with Tamar Granor and Kevin McNeish) of "What's New in Visual FoxPro 7.0". He was the technical editor of "The Hacker's Guide to Visual FoxPro 6.0" and "The Fundamentals". All of these books are available from Hentzenwerke Publishing (www.hentzenwerke.com). He is also the author of "The Visual FoxPro Data Dictionary" in Pinnacle Publishing's Pros Talk Visual FoxPro series. Doug writes the monthly "Reusable Tools" column in FoxTalk.

 

Doug has spoken at every Microsoft FoxPro Developers Conference (DevCon) since 1997 and at user groups and developer conferences all over North America. He has been a Microsoft Most Valuable Professional (MVP) since 1996.
